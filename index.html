About IEEE

The IEEE Computational Intelligence Society (IEEE CIS) is a leading professional organization dedicated to advancing the theory, design, and application of computational intelligence techniques and technologies. It encompasses a broad range of research areas, including artificial intelligence, neural networks, fuzzy systems, evolutionary computation, and machine learning. The IEEE CIS supports the dissemination of knowledge through conferences, publications, and technical activities, fostering collaboration among researchers, practitioners, and educators worldwide. Its mission is to promote innovation and excellence in computational intelligence and to address complex, real-world problems through advanced computational methods

Workshop Details

The IEEE KPRIET CIS students recently hosted an engaging workshop on "Hand on Workshop on Neural Network Trends and Techniques," aiming to provide participants with insights into the latest advancements and methodologies in neural network research. The workshop covered a range of topics, including emerging trends in deep learning architectures, innovative techniques for training and optimizing neural networks, and practical applications of these technologies across various domains. Participants had the opportunity to learn from experts in the field, engage in hands-on sessions, and explore real-world case studies. The event not only highlighted current research developments but also fostered a collaborative environment for sharing knowledge and discussing future directions in neural network technology
